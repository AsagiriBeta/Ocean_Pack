# Ocean Pack — Terra “All-Ocean” Overworld

A **Terra 6.0+** configuration pack that turns the Overworld into a world **dominated by oceans**, aimed at players who want **sailing, diving, island survival**, or the feel of living on a vast sea.

## What you get

- **Oceans as the main stage** — Oceans and deep oceans take up most of the surface; land and coast are rare, so the world reads as “life at sea.”
- **Temperature bands** — Tropical, temperate, cold, and polar-style seas still exist; water color, mood, and feel change as you sail across regions.
- **Warm seas and coral** — Tropical warm waters support richer coral and seabed scenery; some areas lean harder into dense “coral sea” vibes.
- **Warm-ocean-flavored details** — Seagrass, sea pickles, and similar touches are tuned toward **Java warm-ocean** expectations (e.g. warm seas are not treated like kelp-heavy temperate shallows).
- **Underwater cave diving** — More pronounced stone cavities and passages under the seabed, suited to **swim-through exploration and cave-style diving**; shallow coral areas and deep ocean can feel like there’s another world underneath.

## Requirements & notes

- Use with **Terra** on **Minecraft Java Edition**; generation follows Terra’s pipeline.
- **Already-generated chunks do not retroactively change** — use a new world or explore freshly loaded terrain to see the full result.
